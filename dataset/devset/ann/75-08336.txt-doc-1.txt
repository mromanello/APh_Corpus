Results of part of the second phase of excavation of the area known as Sant'Antonio in Cerveteri, conducted by the Consiglio Nazionale delle Ricerche and the Soprintendenza archeologica per l'Etruria meridionale and still in progress.
A more detailed interpretation of the results presented by V. E. Izzet (=> 72-09415) allows us to place the site within the wider historical context of the development of ritual activity in ancient Etruria.
