On the 5th season of the Society for Libyan Studies ' current excavations at the Greek settlement of Euesperides (Benghazi).
A fragmentary Greek inscription was discovered on the mosaic of the penultimate phase.
Outside of the line of the latest defenses, excavation in an ancient quarry ditch revealed a refuse-deposit rich in ceramics and organic materials.
One foreign coin was found, a small 4th-cent. -B. C. bronze of Athens, but non-Cyrenaican coins have proved to be very uncommon on the site.
