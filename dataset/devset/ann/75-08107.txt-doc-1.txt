Interim report on 16 complete barrack plans obtained in excavations at the British northern frontier forts of South Shields and Wallsend in 1986-2001.
This newly obtained information changes our understanding of several aspects of the construction, function, and evolution of auxiliary barracks.
The completely excavated barracks to be considered are listed in chronological order, ranging from the Hadrianic period to the 4th cent.
A. D.
