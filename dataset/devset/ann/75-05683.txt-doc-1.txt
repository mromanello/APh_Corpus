Publication of PDuk. inv. 778 = PRob. inv. 41, from 6th-cent.
Egypt containing, on the recto, LXX, Ps. 90, the heading of Ps. 91 and, on the verso, a version of the Lord 's Prayer and a doxology.
The texts show a number of omissions and variations from the standard textual tradition that always offer inferior readings.
The papyrus was probably used as an amulet.
