Report of the sixth season of excavation carried out by the Society for Libyan Studies. 
 New forms of local black glaze pots were recovered along with fine ware imports from Attica, Corinth, East Greece, south Italy and the Punic world, throwing light on the interrelation between Euesperides and the Mediterranean world from the 5th-3rd cents.
