Muziris, located on the Malabar Coast of India, has been considered one of the most important ports for Indo-Roman trade, but its precise location remains elusive. 
 Pattanam, a settlement dating to the 1st-2nd cents. 
 A.D., may have been part of the legendary Muziris. 
 The increasing abundace of foreign sherds confirms Pattanam's association with the Indo-Roman trade, and the presence of non-local Indian sherds emphasises its wider importance within the Indian context.
