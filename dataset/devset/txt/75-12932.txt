A group of portraits of the imperial period often considered to have been reworked through the chiselling away of all or part of the coiffure and the addition of new updated portions is re-examined in detail. 
 In many cases, the appearance of the portraits can better be explained in technical terms, as a result of initial piecing or later repair. 
 It is doubtful, at the very least, that the images were reworked to bring the coiffures up-to-date.
