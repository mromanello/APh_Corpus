Transcription and discussion of three previously-unread marginal annotations in a 3rd-cent.-A.D. papyrus roll of unknown provenance (PMich. inv. 0.2). 
 The notes are a subsequent addition. 
 They include the longest surviving comment in an ancient text of Demosthenes (9 lines). 
 Their purpose is to facilitate reading ; no new information in supplied. 
 Parallels with scholia are lacking.
