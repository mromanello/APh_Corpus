On the basis of a critical encounter with the recent work by E. Meyer-Zwiffelhoffer (=> 73-12056), underlines some aspects of difference between the administration of « prouinciae populi Romani », on the one hand, and « prouinciae Caesaris » on the other, concentrating on the study of the non-imperial proconsulate of the Imperial period. 
 Main sources studied include Ulpian, « De officio proconsulis », and the s.c. de Cn. 
 Pisone patre (AE 1996, 885)
