A catalogue of Black-Figure vases in the collection of the British School at Athens, including many previously-unpublished examples. 
 The fabrics include Athenian, Corinthian, Boeotian and Euboean. 
 An appendix of largely unpublished Athenian black-figure from the site of Kynosarges, excavated by the British School at Athens (1896-1897), is included.
