Transcription and edition of three new (and one partly unpublished) « Iliad » fragments : PMich. inv. 5694d (Il. 8, 457-478 and 491-507) ; PMich. inv. 1210 + 1216a (Il. 13, 289-301) ; PMich. inv. 1217b (Il. 24, 507-513) ; PMich. inv. 4162b (Il. 24,681-685 and 723-728). Notes on new readings or features of seven « Iliad » papyri from the University of Michigan collection and an « Odyssey » papyrus (PCorn. inv. MSS. A 101.I) from the Cornell collection containing sections of Books 22-23. Republication of « Iliad » and « Odyssey » texts first edited by N. E. Priest (=> 47-01994). Appendix I contains the full text of PMich. inv. 2931 (Il. 2, 1-42), a previously described papyrus. Appendix II is a concordance of all published Homeric papyri from Michigan and Cornell.
