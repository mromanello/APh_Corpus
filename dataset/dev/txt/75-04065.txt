In an approach that takes account of both Plato's « Unwritten philosophy » and his « Dialogues », studies Plato's conception of dialectic, which contains a sketch of the tasks of a Theory of Principles ; then discusses Plato's Metaphysics of the One, with its complementary theses of the transcendence of the Absolute and the nature and existence of a Principle of Multiplicity.
