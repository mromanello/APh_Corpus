Justin appeals to Ge. 
 6, 1-4 to explain the origins of pagan worship, proposing that the progeny of the fallen angels become demons who masquerade as deities and trick pagans into persecuting Christians. 
 Justin's demonization of Greco-Roman religion contrasts both with his approach to Hellenistic philosophy and with his appeal to the disobedience of Adam and Eve to explain Jewish wickedness.
