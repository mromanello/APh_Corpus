On the symbolism in the Mass at Rome of the breaking of the eucharistic bread from the 2nd to 7th cents. 
 A.D. Evidence from Justin, 1 Apol. 65, 5 and 67, 5 ; Hippolytus, Trad. Ap. 23, 5 and 24, 1-2 ; and others. 
 The « fermentum » rite, in which the consecrated bread was sent by the Pope to the priests, is mentioned by Eusebius, HE 5, 24, 14-15 ; the « Liber pontificalis » ; and Innocent I, Epistula ad Decentium Eugubinum 5 (PL 20, 556-557).
