On the « Apocalypse of Paul » (NH 5, 2), an expansion of Paul 's heavenly ascent as described in 2 Cor. 12, 2-4.
The work 's similarities to, and independence from, Irenaeus ' extrapolation of a Valentinian understanding of this ascension in Haer. 2, 30, 7 suggests that both accounts were written in a context of controversy between Valentinian and proto-orthodox Christians wherein both sides tried to claim the apostle Paul 's authority.
