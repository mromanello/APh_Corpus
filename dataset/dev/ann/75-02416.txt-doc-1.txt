Attempts to shed light on the question of the relations between the two earliest versions of the « Hist. Apoll. Regis », RA and RB (both late 5th-early 6th cent. A. D.) by means of an analysis of their « cursus rhymicus », or use of accentual schemes at the end of phrases.
Results tend to confirm that RB is a reelaboration of RA that attempts to smooth out the later work 's irregularities, but it cannot be a direct translation from the Greek
