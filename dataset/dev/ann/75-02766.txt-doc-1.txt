On the literary genre of « Joseph and Aseneth » in particular and the genre of the rewritten Bible in general.
To date, no real scholarly consensus has emerged as to the date and purpose of « Joseph and Aseneth », and it is assumed by many commentators to be based only loosely on the Hebrew Bible.
A detailed examination of the text and of its relationship to the story of Joseph as narrated in Genesis leads to the conclusion that the work is most appropriately classified as an example of a rewritten biblical passage
