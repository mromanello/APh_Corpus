On results of geophysical and topographical survey undertaken at Baccanae, Forum Cassii, and Castellum Amerinum, as part of the Roman Towns in the Middle and Lower Tiber Valley Project of the British School at Rome.
The project is using surface-survey methods to investigate a range of urban sites in the valley downstream from Orte, in order to contribute to our understanding of the nature and variety of Roman urbanism.
This work forms part of the British School 's broader Tiber Valley Project.
