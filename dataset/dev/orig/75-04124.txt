Plato's « Phaedrus » is an unmistakeable response to Isocrates' « Against the sophists ». Consideration of the close parallel between Isocrates' rhetorical method of writing (13, 14-18) and Plato's philosophical method of writing (Phdr. 269ff.) reveals that Plato distinguishes his own method from Isocrates' pseudo-philosophical method (as conceived by Plato).
