In the context of the poet's identification (Met. 5, 464 ff.) of those who gave information about Persephone's disappearance to her mother as Cyane and Arethusa, studies Ovid's playful allusions to and differences from his Greek model, the « Homeric Hymn to Demeter » (2, 22 ff.), as well as his own alternative account in the « Fasti » (4, 575 ff.).
