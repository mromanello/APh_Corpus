The « membra disiecta » of the earliest manuscript of Bede's metrical « Vita S. Cudbercti » may now be listed as follows : Berlin, Staatsbibl. zu Berlin, Preussischer Kulturbesitz, Grimm-Nachlass 132, no. 1 + Budapest, Országos Széchényi Könyvtár, lat. 442 + Budapest, Eötvös Loránd Tudományegyetem Egyetemi Könyvtár, Fragm. lat. 1 + Munich, Stadtarchiv, Historischer Verein Oberbayern, 733/16. The MS was probably written in the 8th cent. A.D. in an Anglo-Saxon center on the Continent rather than in England. It is thus an important new witness to the earliest phase of the continental transmission of Bede's poem.
