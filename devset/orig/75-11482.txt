Contribution to the discussion of H. Hurst and D. Cirone (=> 74-10230), who have presented the results of excavations of a 6th-cent. street on approximately the same alignment as the Neronian street called the « Via Nova » by Lanciani. Includes texts, translations and explanations of literary passages relating to the « noua uia », with the sources divided into three groups : those linking the « noua uia » with 1) the Temple of Vesta ; 2) the Velabrum ; and 3) the Temple of Iuppiter Stator.
