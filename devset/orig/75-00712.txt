However one construes the causal relation between the soul and the body in Aristotle's De an. 2, 2, the systematic relations betweeen types of soul find a biological correlate in the degrees of vital heat in a living organism. Such a parallelism provides a kind of unifying link betwen Aristotle's « De anima » and his biological works. The doctrines of the « De anima » and the biological works do not entail each other, but the structures he articulates in the two inform one another in a systematic way.
