Whereas previous sources for the Armenian campaign of L. Iunius Casennius Paetus were limited to the testimony of Tacitus (Ann. 13, 34-41 ; 14, 23-27 ; 15, 1-17 ; 15, 24-31), it is argued here that the « Lex Portorii » provides evidence for the dating of Casennius Paetus' Armenian campaign, placing it in the autumn of 61. This affects the entire chronology of events on the eastern border of the Roman Empire during Nero's principate
