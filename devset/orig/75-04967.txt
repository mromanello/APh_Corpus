It is probably impossible to reconstruct the major philosophical positions defended by the historical Socrates by relying on our principle witnesses for Socrates. But even if the « Socratic question » is a problem incapable of solution, it is nevertheless important to reexamine and reappropriate the texts that first raised the question, since these texts continue to shape our representation of Socrates in a way mostly unperceived by us. F. E. D. Schleiermacher's « Ueber den Werth des Sokrates als Philosophen » (1815) is the work that most contributed to formulating the question.
