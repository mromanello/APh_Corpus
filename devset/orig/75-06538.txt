In continuation of a previous work (=> 59-05226), examines the episode in the legend of Cacus that occurs in the sequel to Heracles' theft of the cattle of Geryon. Aspects studied include Cacus as captured seer, Cacus as captured death-demon or « undead », and the ambivalence of both Cacus and Heracles. Appendix p. 38-39 : Cacus in Livy 1.7.7.
